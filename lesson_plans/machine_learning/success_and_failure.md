
# Success and Failure of Machine Learning

I have no idea how to currently use this material but I wanted to put it somewhere.

## Successful use cases

- filter spam email
- sort letters by postal codes (see MNIST dataset)
- translate web pages
- mark cancer cells on a tissue sample
- generate images for teaching
- movie/book recommenders (Netflix Prize)
- driving assistants
- recognize objects in an image
- sort items on a conveyor belt (good/bad fruit or recyclable material)
- Alpha Go
- Hexagon Machine on Spotify

## Spectacular Failures

- image of person labeled "gorilla"
- Tay chatbot by Microsoft
- hand dryer that only works for white people
- predictive policing in the U.S.
- Cambridge Analytica Scandal
- Pope in white blazer
- GPT: "I need to wash my car. The car wash is 100m away. Should I walk or drive?"
- "Towers of Hanoi Paper"
