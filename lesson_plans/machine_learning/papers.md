
# Paper Club

## Lesson Goal:

Students read a paper about an AI milestone and present it within 10 minutes.

## Time Frame:

1-2 hours.

## Content Delivery:

Display the task.

    Read the paper, understand the and prepare an understandable summary of up to 10 minutes.

    Focus on:

    - what is the paper about?
    - what should you remember from it
    - what do you find difficult?
    - is there any code you could run yourself?

Then share the papers. Let people work in groups of 2-4.
Balance the groups so that you end up with a maximum of 7 presentations.

## Material:

Ask Kristian for his collection of "best of" AI papers.

## Comments:

This needs to be clearly moderated. I suggest using a timing device that is visible to all. 
While students prepare, sort out connection issues with a screen (or use an online meeting room for sharing).

Sometimes students are not used to other students speaking and start misbehaving. A circle setup (no tables) helps with that.

Depending on how much you trust the students, reading a paper can of course be done as homework.
