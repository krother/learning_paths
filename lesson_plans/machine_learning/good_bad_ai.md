
# Good and Bad AI

## Goal

Participants reflect on benefits and risks of using AI.

## Time

30'

## How to run the activity

Write two questions on the board: "What should AI do to make our life:"

- LEFT: healthy, peaceful & prosperous
- RIGHT: completely miserable

Task the group to collect ideas. Randomly assign participants to groups of 3-4 people. Let them discuss for 5-7 minutes.

Then ask them for suggestions. Collect them to on the board. Repeat but do not comment.

## Comments

This activity can be done at the very beginning or towards the end of a course.
Towards the end of a lesson it fits as well if the group runs low on energy.

Right now (03/2026) I have the impression that many people in IT are a bit afraid of the AI transition.
Sharing concerns and thoughts with other people actually helps. If a deeper discussion results, I would not mind letting it run for a while.
